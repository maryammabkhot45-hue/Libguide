About the Library

The Al Wasl University Library supports academic excellence by providing access to high-quality information resources and a supportive learning environment. Our services help students and faculty improve research skills, locate academic materials, and develop information literacy.

We aim to empower users to become confident, independent researchers who can find, evaluate, and use information effectively in their studies and future careers.

Library Mission

  • Provide reliable digital and print resources
  • Support teaching, learning, and research
  • Promote information literacy skills
  • Encourage independent and lifelong learning

How to Search

Follow these steps to search effectively and find accurate, reliable information for your assignments and research projects.

1. Identify Keywords

Start by choosing the main words and phrases that represent your research topic. Think of synonyms and related terms you might also use.

2. Use Boolean Operators

  • AND â narrows your results (e.g., libraries AND UAE)
  • OR â broadens your results (e.g., students OR learners)
  • NOT â excludes terms (e.g., reference NOT law)

3. Apply Filters

Use database filters to limit your search by date, subject area, document type, language, or availability of full text.

4. Evaluate Sources

Always check the author, publication year, journal or publisher, and the academic level of the source to ensure it is suitable for university work.

5. Save, Download, or Export

Most databases allow you to download PDFs, save items to a personal folder, or export citations to reference managers.
